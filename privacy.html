Pacific Facade Privacy Policy

Effective date: May 7, 2026

Information We Collect

When you request an estimate, we collect the information you submit, including your name, phone number, email address if provided, ZIP code, project description and uploaded photos.

We may also collect basic attribution data such as page URL, referrer, UTM parameters and advertising click identifiers so we can understand which marketing campaigns produced the request.

How We Use Information

We use your information to review your stucco or facade repair request, contact you about the project, coordinate next steps and improve our website and advertising performance.

Sharing

Pacific Facade is a project intake and referral service. We may share your request details with independent local repair partners so they can review the project, provide pricing or schedule a site visit.

We may also use service providers for website hosting, lead delivery, email, messaging, analytics and advertising measurement.

Advertising And Analytics

We may use tools such as Google Analytics, Google Ads and Google Tag Manager to measure site visits, form submissions and advertising performance. These tools may use cookies or similar technologies.

Your Choices

You can ask us to update or delete your lead information by contacting us at leads@pacificfacade.com. You can also use your browser settings to limit cookies.

Contact

For privacy questions, contact Pacific Facade at leads@pacificfacade.com.
